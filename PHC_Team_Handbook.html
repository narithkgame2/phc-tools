Task Manager
Your personal to-do list for PHC — see what's due, what's overdue, and mark things done. Everyone has their own tab.
→Who uses it: Everyone — Nick, Monika, Reza, and Marina each have a tab.
→What it replaces: chasing each other on Telegram to ask "did you do X?"

1

Left sidebar — click your tab to see only your tasks.
2

Lead Tracker
Every person who's shown interest lives here. This is where a name becomes a client — or gets lost, if nobody updates it.
→Who uses it: Monika, Reza, Marina — anyone talking to a lead.
→Stages: New Lead → Contacted → Viewing → Negotiating → Closed (or Hold / Released).

How to use it
1
Open "Board" view
Leads shown as cards, grouped by stage.
2
Click a card to log what happened
Add a note every time you call, message, or meet the lead — this is the lead's whole history.
3
Drag the card when the stage changes
Moved from "Contacted" to "Viewing"? Drag it there — don't leave it stale.
4
New lead came in on WhatsApp or in person?
Click "+ New Lead" and add it manually — it won't appear here on its own.
5
Check "Overdue" every morning
Sidebar tab — shows every lead with no follow-up date set. Set one for each.
Why this matters
The Lead Alert bot pings Nick the moment a new lead comes in — but only this tool tracks what happens after. If it's not logged here, the deal's history only lives in your head.
