Where the ROI actually comes from

Drives more leads

The 70/30 educational-to-listing content mix, and posting on the platform JP/DE buyers actually use (LinkedIn). Both are free regardless of Buffer tier — they're editorial decisions, not tooling.

Doesn't, by itself

Upgrading to Essentials. Paying for more channels or analytics doesn't fix a content mix that's currently 100% listings — it just automates the same low-conversion posts faster.

”

Buffer's tier is an efficiency layer, not a lead-generation lever. Free tier removes zero capability PHC needs at 3 channels — the fix for "100+ posts → 2 leads" is the content mix, which costs the same either way. Spend the $0 now; spend the $15/mo later, once there's data worth analyzing.

When to actually upgrade
1Adding a 4th channel (TikTok or YouTube Shorts) once video content exists
2Need analytics to prove the 70/30 mix is actually improving lead flow, not just a hunch
3Mey needs to queue content without routing through one shared login
4Post volume regularly exceeds 10 queued items per channel
