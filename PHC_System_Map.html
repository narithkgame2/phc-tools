Content Bot — @PHC_Content_Bot
LIVE
What it does
Nick forwards a listing (his own channel, another agent's channel, ChatGPT text, or plain "Label: Value" text) to the bot. It detects the format across 5 tiers, rebuilds it into PHC's template, translates to EN/JP/RU/DE, and sends one Approve-All review message — a single tap posts to all 4 pilot channels.
70 / 30 Content Rule
70% Educational — market insights, ROI guides, investor stories, why Cambodia
30% Listing — property features, unit types, pricing, floor plans
Not yet applied — pilot channels are still nearly all listing content. See Focus Next panel above.
Input → Output
IN: Forwarded/pasted listing text
OUT: One Approve-All review → 4 Telegram channels
LANGUAGES: EN · JP · RU · DE
→ Nothing posts without Nick's tap
