How this is scored: each system-map section is broken into its individual components. A component counts as Live only if it runs without a person re-triggering it each time (a bot, a script, a scheduled job) — this measures "shipped," not "finished." An item can be Live and still have an amber comment underneath flagging what's rough, unoptimized, or not yet in real use — read those before trusting the percentage at face value. Manual by design covers steps that are inherently human — collecting a signature, confirming a receipt — and isn't counted against progress, since there's nothing to "finish" there. Only the ratio of Live vs. Planned reflects real backlog. The overall ring above is a simple average across the 6 sections.
